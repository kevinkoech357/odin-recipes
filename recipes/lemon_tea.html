Honey-Lemon Ginger Tea

Description

Honey Lemon Ginger Tea is a gentle and soothing herbal infusion of grated ginger and fresh lemon with honey as a natural sweetener.

It is a healthy and invigorating drink that is often consumed to alleviate flu or cold symptoms, or as a delicious warm drink during those cold winter days. This revitalizing tea has a relaxing and calming effect and boosts the immune system as well.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup grated ginger root
  • 3 tea bags
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 3 tablespoons honey

Steps

  1. Stir water, brown sugar, and grated ginger root together in a saucepan; bring to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, and cook at a simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Remove saucepan from heat and add tea bags; steep tea to desired strength, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove and discard tea bags.
  3. Stir lemon juice and honey into the tea; strain into a pitcher.
